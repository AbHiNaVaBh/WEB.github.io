Model Polytechnic College

The Model Polytechnic College,Vadakara is the brain child of intitute of Human Resources Development ( I H R D ) established by the Govt. of Kerala in the year 1988,whose main objective is to function as a catalyst to foster the growth of electronics ,computer and specialised feilds such as Medical Electronics throw a plethora of innovative endeavours.
The Polytechnic College offers three year Diploma cource in applied electronics, Computer hardware maintanance and Medical Electronics,the courses being recognised by the PSC.The institution has been accredited by the National Board of Aaccreditation (NBA) of the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) New Delhi.

3 Year Diploma

Computer Hardware Engineering

Computer hardware engineering involves designing, developing, and testing computer systems, components, and peripherals. It is an exciting field that allows students to combine their interests in technology and problem-solving to create innovative and efficient hardware solutions. Computer hardware engineers work in a variety of industries, including computer systems design, electronics manufacturing, and telecommunications, and play a critical role in advancing the capabilities of modern technology. Pursuing a career in computer hardware engineering can lead to opportunities for creativity, intellectual challenge, and significant contributions to the tech industry.

Total :60 Seats

Biomedical Engineering

Biomedical engineering is a dynamic and innovative field that combines the principles of biology, medicine, and engineering to design and develop new medical technologies and devices. As a biomedical engineer, you could help create life-saving devices like pacemakers, artificial organs, and prosthetics, or develop new diagnostic tools that can detect diseases earlier and more accurately. You could work in hospitals, research labs, or medical device companies, and your work could have a profound impact on the lives of patients
around the world. If you're interested in making a difference in healthcare and using your scientific and technical skills to improve people's lives, then biomedical engineering could be the perfect career for you.

Total :60 Seats

Electronics Engineering

Electronic engineering is a fascinating and dynamic field that deals with the design, development, and maintenance of electronic systems and devices. As an electronic engineering student, you will learn how to create and improve upon technologies that power our modern world, from smartphones and computers to healthcare equipment and satellites. You will gain a deep understanding of electronics, circuits, computer hardware, programming languages, and much more. This program provides a solid foundation in math, physics, and computer science, and also allows you to specialize in areas such as signal processing, telecommunications, robotics, and nanotechnology. Pursuing a degree in electronic engineering offers you exciting opportunities to shape the future of technology and make a positive impact on society.

Total :60 Seats

Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Electrical and electronic engineering is a dynamic and innovative field that focuses on the design, development, and maintenance of electrical and electronic devices and systems. Students who choose to study electrical and electronic engineering will be exposed to a wide range of exciting and challenging topics, such as digital signal processing, power systems, control systems, microelectronics, communication systems, and more. With the increasing demand for energy-efficient and eco-friendly technologies, the career prospects for electrical and electronic engineers are very promising. This field offers students the opportunity to develop cutting-edge solutions to real-world problems, and make a positive impact on the world. If you have a passion for science, technology, and innovation, and want to make a difference in the world, then electrical and electronic engineering is the perfect choice for you.

Total :60 Seats
